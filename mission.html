Our Mission

Sport is most entertaining when played competitively at a high level. This is true for both players and spectators. Here at FindMyCoach, our mission is simple. We exist in order to connect the UK's coaches with it's aspiring athletes looking to take their game to the next level - whatever game that may be. We believe that supporting and nurturing these connections will create an ecosystem in which the quality of sport in the UK will improve exponentially. Consequently, this will provide value to all stakeholders of the sporting environment in this country.

For The Coaches

Our aim is to become the largest database of coaches in the UK. By providing coaches with a geographically-based sport-specific platform, we can greatly increase the ability for you to market your services effectively. By centralising the huge number of coaching professionals, FindMyCoach will inevitably appeal to the nearly 10 million adult participants actively receiving coaching in the UK, as well as to those who hold an interest but were previously inactive due to a lack of accessibility. The clients you book through the platform will be able to leave feedback for the session, so you can showcase your quality and client satisfaction to anyone who comes across your profile.

For The Athletes

We want to provide athletes of all abilities with access to the abundance of specialist sports coaching that the UK has to offer. We know it can be tiresome and difficult not only choosing, but finding the right coach for your requirements. FindMyCoach aims to provide you with all viable coaching options based on a number of metrics, all in one place. Our geographical search will present you with the most logical and reasonable choices based on the specific inputs you provide us with. All coaches will be DBS vetted and any qualifications they have will be verified, so you can ensure that you are getting the exact service you are looking for.
